Client summary

Boutique property management company in Lake Oconee, Georgia needs a Twilio developer to take an already-completed AI phone assistant live. The Claude-powered assistant, call script, routing logic, and scenario documentation are already prepared. The remaining work is the Twilio Programmable Voice integration, live transfer setup, business-hours routing, and end-to-end testing.

Project goals

Connect the existing business phone number to Twilio Programmable Voice so inbound calls route into the AI assistant. Implement reliable live transfer flows for emergency maintenance and property management inquiries. Apply weekday business-hours logic for Monday through Friday, 9:00 AM to 4:00 PM, with different handling after hours. Validate each documented call scenario before handoff.
